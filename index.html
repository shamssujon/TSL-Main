A Dedication to
Excellence

For over 23 years, Technological Solutions Limited's excellence in the application of science to the productive sector has transformed the Caribbean region's agribusiness sector.

This was the brainchild of TSL's Chief Executive Officer, who pioneered the development of a centre devoted to the application of Food (and Related) Sciences to enhance food safety, quality and competitiveness in the Caribbean, and developing businesses trading in the global marketplace.
