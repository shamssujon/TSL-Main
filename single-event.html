Eventbrite Tickets Demo

March 22, 2023 @ 7:00 pm - March 25, 2023 @ 10:00 pm

Free
  • « Event in a Series
Eventbrite Tickets can import events (and their tickets) from Eventbrite into The Events Calendar.

Eventbrite is an extremely popular service for selling tickets. In fact, some prefer using the service for selling tickets because of its simplicity and hands-off approach to ticketing.

Whether you use Eventbrite or some other service, no one likes having to manage the same thing in multiple places and that what Eventbrite Tickets solves. Even though you have an event on Eventbrite, you might still want it to show up in the calendar on your own website. Eventbrite Tickets can import events (and their tickets) from Eventbrite into The Events Calendar.

Better yet, it even does the inverse and lets you publish an event on your calendar and instantly sync it with Eventbrite. Now you have the best of both worlds where your event is managed and maintained on your own calendar while you get to use Eventbrite’s ticketing capabilities!

Events that are imported to or synced with Eventbrite will display a ticket form on the event page, like the example below.

  • Google Calendar
  • iCalendar
  • Outlook 365
  • Outlook Live
