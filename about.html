About TSL

Effectively combining science and business, for the past 20 years Technological Solutions Limited (TSL) has been revolutionising food safety and quality control systems, not just in Jamaica, but across the Caribbean region.

Our History

Technological Solutions Limited (TSL) began operations in July 1996, and has since built an enviable reputation for its work in preparing organizations to deal with the practical realities of competing and exporting in the globalized business environment.

In 2019 TSL expanded its operations by establishing two new companies, TSL Laboratory Services Limited (TSL LS) and TSL Consulting and Compliance Limited (TSL CC), as subsidiaries, to join the existing TSL Audit Services Limited (TASL) in the TSL group.

The related TSL Certification Services International Limited (TSL CSI) was also incorporated in 2019. TSL CSI is the first, and currently, only Caribbean based operation to have been granted a licence by the Safe Quality Food Institute (SQFI) to offer SQF Certification, which it offers along with HACCP and GMP certification. Another related entity TSL Technical Services Limited (TTSL) works closely with the TSL group in the design and implementation of projects.

Range of Services

Led by Dr. André Gordon, the TSL group of companies offers a comprehensive range of technical services to support the productive and hospitality sectors in Jamaica and the Caribbean. It does this by utilising the skills of its highly competent staff to meet its customers needs. The TSL Team provides guidance in trade-related technical requirements and specializes in:

  • Food safety
  • Quality assurance
  • Occupational health and safety
  • Auditing
  • Training
  • Production systems design and implementation
  • Analytical services
  • Inclusive of shelf life analysis
  • Consumer/product complaint evaluation
  • Plant and facility layout and design
  • New product development
  • Profit improvement
  • Troubleshooting and consulting
